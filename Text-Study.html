Text Study

Writing systems and their articulation sit at the intersection of technology, language, and culture. The ability to reproduce these systems is integral to deeply-rooted cultural notions regarding memory, power, history, the nation-state, personal identity, media and definitions of authority and knowledge.

In this course, I tried several ways of artistic expression. Graphic design, AR, and sound visualization through touch designer, to explore the limit of the original text and my response connection space.

The Combination of Response and Original Text

Original Text and Response Combination Work

I tried to use Touch Desinger to visualization voice. 
There are two voice in this project, one is google translate reading the original text, the other one is my voice of personal response to the original text.

Touch Desinger Working Process

I visually visualize the sound of my response reading and the sound of google translate AI reading the original text in Touch Designer. I ended up splitting the video into two screens to play at the same time. My response is a circular, net-like, symmetrical 2D graphic, which will be drawn into a pupil-like circle after repeated audio loops. I call it the eyes of God, watching the behavior of believers all the time.
